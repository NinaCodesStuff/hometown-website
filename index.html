Visit Greenfield

Explore this humble little town rich in history and heritage located just East of Indianapolis.

Top three activities to do in Greenfield

Tour a local museum

Visit historical sites

Pick apples at an orchard

The James Whitcomb Riley Home Museum is home of the author of the famous work known as "Little Orphan Annie".

The Hancock County Courthouse is a famous historical site known for it's impressive architecture and historical significance.

Tuttle Orchards is a great place to have some fun with friends and family and pick delicous apples.
